# Cold Spring & Walkway Procedures

**Location:** Cold Spring Area / Walkway  
**Displayed:** After exceeding time limit or experiencing perceptual instability  

---

1. **Remain in the cold spring for a full 5 minutes.**  
   Shorter durations may not produce the intended effect.

2. **While cooling, avoid visual inspection of other visitors.**  
   Focus on breathing and remain still.

3. **Wet surfaces may cause slipping.**  
   Walk slowly and watch your footing when moving between areas.

---

*Loss of clarity is temporary.*
