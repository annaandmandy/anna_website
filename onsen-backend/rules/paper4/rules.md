# Injury Response Protocol

**Location:** Near the hottest pool  
**Displayed:** Only after injury is detected  

---

1. **If you are injured, return immediately to the hot spring area.**  
   Locate the pool with the highest temperature.

2. **Remain still in the water.**  
   Do not attempt to treat the injury yourself.

3. **Staff members dressed in black will arrive to assist you.**  
   Do not call out to them. They are aware of your situation.

---

*This procedure is for your safety.*
